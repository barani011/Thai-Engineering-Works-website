ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Thai Engineering Works is a Coir Board–approved manufacturer of all kinds of coir processing machines, serving customers across India and overseas. Established in 1985 and backed by over 40 years of successful business experience, the company is led by Proprietor Mr. S. SIVARAMA KRISHNAN. We specialize in designing and building durable, high-performance machinery that increases productivity and reduces manual labor in coir production. Our product range includes Fiber Extraction Machines, Conveyor Systems, Screener Machines, Baling Machines, Pith Processing Units, and fully customized coir-fiber manufacturing setups. With a strong focus on quality, innovation, timely delivery, and reliable after-sales support, we continue to be a trusted partner for coir manufacturers seeking efficiency and long-term performance.
